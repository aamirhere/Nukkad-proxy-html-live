Gul's moving parts (blink / talk)

Give me clean transparent cut-outs — eyes-closed and beak-open. No solid background: the studio feathers the edges so each part melts into the face (no box, nothing hidden). On each beat you place them once (drag / size / rotate / blend), and blink & talk stamp them on top — no auto-detection. Reusable across every post.

Eyes-closed: include a little of the surrounding face skin so it covers the open eye. Beak-open: a clean cut-out of just the open beak (a touch of chin is fine) — placed over her beak it merges. The “blend” slider softens the edges.
